# Version Plan — Royal 3D Runner

## v1 — Foundations
- ✅ Three-lane movement with keyboard controls.
- ✅ Perspective rendering on canvas with royal palette.
- ✅ Obstacle spawning, collision, restart flow.
- ✅ Basic score timer.

- ✅ Crown pickups that increase score and fill a charge meter.

- ✅ Difficulty scaling tied to survival time.
- ✅ Streak multiplier and streak HUD.
- ✅ Pause/resume, audio stub toggle, clearer failure messaging.

## Next Steps
- Add mobile touch controls (swipe lanes, tap charge).
- Persist highscores per version.
